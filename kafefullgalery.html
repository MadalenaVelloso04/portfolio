Personal Project 2024

Project Overview

This project explores a playful brand identity for K-afe, a Korean cafe that brings a unique blend of tradition and fun to its customers. Centered around a cheerful mascot, the brand aims to create a lighthearted, worry-free atmosphere, inviting visitors to step away from the stresses of daily life. In a society where work and reputation are highly valued, K-afe serves as a refreshing escape, where patrons can enjoy a moment of relaxation and joy.

Concept and Creation
of the Brand

The mascot embodies the cafe's welcoming spirit, building a friendly relationship with customers and embodying the easy-going, carefree essence of K-afe. This approach brings a sense of warmth and approachability to the brand, setting it apart from more formal environments. drawing from its iconic patterns and national hues to create a welcoming, joyful atmosphere for our guests.

Concept and Creation of the menu

The menu design reflects this fun, inviting atmosphere, featuring both traditional Korean pastries and popular international treats. Bright, warm colors are used throughout the brand to foster a cozy, uplifting ambiance that encourages customers to unwind and embrace the moment. This was a delightful project developed for personal growth, with the aim of blending cultural authenticity and a fresh, inviting experience for K-afe's visitors.
